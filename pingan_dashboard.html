★ Investment Portfolio Deep-Dive 投资组合深度分析

The Hidden Value Engine

Why This Section Matters for Ping An's Intrinsic Value 为何投资组合决定平安内在价值

For an insurance company, the investment portfolio is the primary source of intrinsic value above book. Ping An's RMB 6.49 trillion insurance funds are deployed as "float" (保险浮存金) — Buffett's favorite form of leverage: capital that policyholders pay the company to hold. Every 50bps improvement in comprehensive yield = ~RMB 32 billion of incremental pre-tax income, equivalent to ~6% of Ping An's annual operating profit. This is why the market's focus on headline P/E misses the real story.

Asset Allocation (End-2025 est.) 资产配置

Stock allocation raised to 14.8% at year-end 2025, up +7.2 pps from year-end 2024 — the highest equity ratio among China's five listed insurers. Ping An deployed an estimated RMB 475B of fresh capital into equities during 2025.

Portfolio Growth (RMB Trillion) 投资组合规模演变

Portfolio has compounded at 12–14% annually through net premium inflows and investment gains. Management targets continued expansion under the "Double Barbell" (双杠铃) strategy balancing long-duration bonds and risk assets.

The "Double Barbell" Strategy 双杠铃资产配置策略

Ping An's CIO Benjamin Deng has articulated a dual-ended allocation framework:

End #1 — Long-duration Interest Rate Bonds

China government bonds, local government bonds, policy bank bonds. Purpose: match 12-year liability duration; lock in yields when rates are high. In 2025 the group aggressively front-loaded bond purchases as 10-yr China govt yields fell to ~1.7%.

End #2 — Risk Assets

High-dividend blue-chip equities (>4% yield), growth tech (AI, semis, renewables), real estate debt, infrastructure ABS, private equity. Purpose: enhance returns above liability cost in a low-rate environment.

The FVOCI "Silent Reservoir" FVOCI 隐形储备

57% of Ping An's equity holdings are classified as FVOCI — Fair Value through Other Comprehensive Income rather than P&L. Over RMB 90 billion of pre-tax unrealized gains sit in OCI, bypassing current net profit but building book value.

57%的股票持仓列为FVOCI（以公允价值计量且其变动计入其他综合收益），超过RMB 900亿的税前未实现收益未计入当期损益，形成"隐形储备"。

Investment Yield History (Net vs Comprehensive) 投资收益率历史

The gap between realized yields and the EV long-run assumption of 4.0% represents a hidden margin of safety — Ping An's embedded value is calculated on conservative assumptions, meaning economic EV likely exceeds reported EV.

Why the Bank Accumulation Strategy Works 银行股加仓逻辑

The arbitrage:
  • 10-yr China government bond yield: ~1.7%
  • ICBC H-share dividend yield: ~6.4%
  • Effective spread locked in: ~470 bps
  • Classified as FVOCI → price volatility absorbed in OCI
  • New IFRS 9 rules allow this treatment for equity investments held long-term
Regulatory tailwind:
  • National Financial Regulatory Administration may allow shareholders to be "major investors" (>5% stake) in up to 4 banks (currently limited to 2)
  • Ping An, via its subsidiary Ping An Bank, is effectively blocked from further bank stakes — rule change would unlock new flexibility
  • JPMorgan analysis: potential beneficiaries include ICBC, CCB, PSBC, China Merchants Bank

  • Change in co-CEO structure creating strategic uncertainty
  • Price exceeds HK$100 without fundamental improvement (valuation discipline)

The Bottom Line 核心结论

Ping An is not just a Chinese insurance company. It is a RMB 14 trillion balance-sheet conglomerate whose real intrinsic value rests on three under-appreciated pillars: (1) a RMB 6.49 trillion institutional investment portfolio earning above-assumption returns; (2) a wide-moat integrated finance flywheel with 251 million customers and 99% retention; (3) a healthcare ecosystem that is quietly becoming China's largest managed care operator.

Buying below HK$65 offers the Graham combination of safety of principal (1.05× book, 4.7% dividend, wide moat) and adequate return (30-50% upside to intrinsic value). This is what "wonderful company at fair price" looks like in the HK market.
