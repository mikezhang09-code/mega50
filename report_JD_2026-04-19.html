Executive Summary · 执行摘要

JD.com is a classic "fog of war" value situation: a fundamentally healthy core business obscured by a large, uncertain capital deployment in food delivery.

The Positive Case

  • JD Retail (81% of revenue) grew operating income 25% YoY in 2025, margin expanded 4.0% → 4.6%.
  • Fortress balance sheet: ~$22B net cash = 45% of market cap.
  • Aggressive capital return: 6.3% of shares retired in 2025 + $1.4B dividend = ~10% total shareholder yield.
  • Service revenue mix (higher-margin) growing 23.6% YoY — structural margin tailwind.

The Risk Case 风险情景

  • New Businesses lost RMB 46.6B in 2025 — primarily food delivery war vs. Meituan.
  • Consolidated operating margin collapsed 3.3% → 0.2%.
  • Free cash flow fell from RMB 43.7B → RMB 6.5B.
  • Munger institutional imperative: is management chasing a market they cannot economically win?
Triangulated base-case fair value: ~US$52 / ADS (range $41 floor to $74 SOTP). Versus current $33.50, implied margin of safety is ~36% to base case, ~120% to SOTP upside case. 基准估值约52美元,相对当前33.50美元有约36%安全边际。

Business Overview · 业务概览

JD.com operates three reportable segments: JD Retail (core e-commerce), JD Logistics (own + 3rd-party logistics), and New Businesses (food delivery, overseas, Jingxi, JD Property).

Unlike Alibaba: First-Party Model 自营模式

JD holds inventory, operates warehouses, and runs last-mile delivery. This is structurally more capital-intensive than Alibaba's pure marketplace, but creates a higher-trust proposition that has carved out a durable niche — especially in electronics, healthcare, and categories where counterfeit risk matters.

Revenue Mix Trend (positive structural story) 收入结构 —— 正向趋势

  • Net product revenues FY2025: RMB 1,023.8B (+10.3% YoY)
  • Net service revenues FY2025: RMB 285.3B (+23.6% YoY)
  • Within service: Marketplace & marketing +18.9%; Logistics & other services +26.6%
  • Service revenues carry near-zero COGS drag, driving structural margin expansion
