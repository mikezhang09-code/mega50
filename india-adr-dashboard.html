⚠️ Important for US-Based Investors

Only ~9 Indian companies trade directly as ADRs on US exchanges. Major companies like Reliance Industries, TCS, Bharti Airtel, Bajaj Finance, and Asian Paints have no ADR/US listing. To access these, consider India-focused ETFs such as INDA (iShares MSCI India), SMIN (iShares MSCI India Small-Cap), or EPI (WisdomTree India Earnings). ADR returns trail INR returns by ~3-4% annually due to rupee depreciation — an important consideration for dollar-denominated portfolios.
