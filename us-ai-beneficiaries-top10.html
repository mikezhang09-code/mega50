Portfolio Logic: Picks & Shovels Across Every Chokepoint
组合逻辑：覆盖每一个关键瓶颈的 "卖铲人" 策略

The AI inference explosion requires compute (NVIDIA, Broadcom), which requires fabrication (TSMC) and memory (SK Hynix). These chips must be networked (Arista, Coherent) inside facilities that need power (Constellation Energy) and cooling (Vertiv). The resulting compute generates tokens consumed by enterprise applications (ServiceNow) running on hyperscaler infrastructure (Microsoft).

SK Hynix at ~5x forward earnings stands out as the most striking valuation anomaly — a company with 58% operating margins and sold-out HBM capacity trading at a discount reserved for cyclical commodity businesses. ServiceNow and Microsoft both show significant valuation compression from recent selloffs, with analyst targets implying 60-100% upside. The key macro risk: hyperscaler capex projected at $660-720 billion in 2026 must prove sustainable.
