Business Summary / 业务概要

Alibaba is the world's largest e-commerce company by GMV, operating China's dominant Taobao (C2C) and Tmall (B2C) marketplaces. The company is undergoing a strategic pivot under CEO Eddie Wu's "user first, AI-driven" strategy, heavily investing in AI + Cloud (RMB 120B+ capex in 4 quarters) and quick commerce (Taobao Instant Commerce). Alibaba Cloud is China's #1 public cloud provider with 36% revenue growth and 10 consecutive quarters of triple-digit AI revenue growth. The Qwen AI platform has surpassed 300M MAU. Core commerce faces intensifying competition from PDD/Temu, JD, and Douyin, while the AI pivot suppresses near-term profitability and FCF.

阿里巴巴是全球最大的电子商务公司，运营中国主导的淘宝和天猫平台。公司正在进行AI战略转型，大力投资AI+云计算基础设施和即时零售业务。 阿里云是中国最大的公有云服务商，AI相关产品收入连续十个季度保持三位数增长。核心电商面临拼多多、京东和抖音的激烈竞争。
