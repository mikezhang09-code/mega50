Chapter I · 第一章
A Textile Mill and a Grudge
一家纺织厂与一场怨恨 (1839–1965)

Berkshire Hathaway's corporate lineage stretches back to 1839, when Oliver Chace established a textile mill in Rhode Island. The Hathaway Manufacturing Company was founded in 1888. In 1955, the two merged into Berkshire Hathaway — 12,000 employees, 15 plants, $53M net worth. By 1964, only two mills remained. Net worth had collapsed to $22 million.

伯克希尔·哈撒韦的企业谱系可追溯到1839年。1955年两家企业合并，拥有12,000名员工、15家工厂、净资产约5,300万美元。到1964年，只剩两家工厂，净资产缩水至约2,200万美元。

In 1962, a 31-year-old Warren Buffett began buying shares at $7.50. When Seabury Stanton shorted the agreed tender price by one-eighth of a dollar — from $11.50 to $11.375 — Buffett was so enraged he bought control of the entire company out of spite. By May 10, 1965, he had enough shares to seize the board.

1962年，31岁的巴菲特以每股约7.50美元开始买入。当斯坦顿将约定价格少了八分之一美元时，巴菲特怒不可遏，出于赌气买下了整个公司的控制权。

"我买过的最愚蠢的股票——不是因为亏了钱，而是因为惊人的机会成本。"
— Warren Buffett 沃伦·巴菲特

Chapter VII · 第七章
The Float Flywheel
浮存飞轮——伯克希尔的金融引擎
Insurance Float Growth: $17M → $174B (1967–2025)
保险浮存增长：1,700万 → 1,740亿美元

Float grew at a 16–18% compound annual rate over five decades. The mechanism is deceptively simple: premiums collected today fund claims paid years or decades later, with the intervening capital invested in stocks, bonds, and whole companies. When underwriting is profitable, the float is better than free — because the insurer is being paid to hold other people's money.

浮存在五十年间以16-18%的年复合增长率增长。机制看似简单：今天收取的保费用于支付数年或数十年后的理赔，其间的资金投资于股票、债券和整个企业。当承保盈利时，浮存比免费还好——因为保险公司实际上是在收取费用来持有别人的钱。

Conclusion · 结论
The Machine Beyond the Maker
超越创造者的机器

Berkshire's transformation from a $22 million textile wreck into a $1+ trillion conglomerate rests on an interlocking system of structural advantages: insurance float providing hundreds of billions in free capital, decentralized culture attracting exceptional managers, and permanent-capital structure enabling patient compounding.

伯克希尔从净资产2,200万美元的纺织业残骸转变为超过万亿美元的企业集团，依靠的是一套相互联锁的结构性优势：保险浮存提供数千亿美元免费资本、去中心化文化吸引优秀管理者、永久资本结构使耐心的长期复利成为可能。

"Compounding is not magic but discipline."

"复利不是魔法，而是纪律。"
