Business Summary 业务概要

NetEase is China's second-largest gaming company (after Tencent), with a portfolio spanning long-running MMORPG franchises (Fantasy Westward Journey), globally successful titles (Marvel Rivals, Naraka: Bladepoint), and growing verticals in music streaming (Cloud Music), education (Youdao), and e-commerce (Yanxuan). FY2025 revenue reached RMB 112.6B (~$16.1B), up 7% YoY, with games contributing ~82% of total revenue. The company sits on a fortress balance sheet with RMB 163.5B (~$23B) in net cash.

Formula: √(22.5 × EPS × BVPS)

Note: Graham Number undervalues high-ROIC, asset-light businesses. NetEase's value is in its earnings power, not its book value. This sets a conservative floor.

Method 2: Earnings Power Value 盈利能力价值

Normalized EPS (5yr avg): ~$7.00 per ADS

Reverse DCF: What's Priced In? 反向贴现：市场隐含增长率

At $113 per ADS, with 10% discount rate and $10.10 FCF/share, the market is pricing in approximately 3–4% perpetual growth. Given NetEase's 7-15% historical growth rate and expanding global footprint (Marvel Rivals, Where Winds Meet), this implied growth is conservative.

Key Insight 关键洞察: The market is essentially valuing NetEase as a slow-growth utility, ignoring its (1) AI integration across gaming, (2) global expansion trajectory, (3) massive cash pile optionality. This creates opportunity for patient value investors.

Economic Moat Assessment 经济护城河评估

1. Intangible Assets / 无形资产 — 3/3

Massive IP portfolio: Fantasy Westward Journey (20+ years), Onmyoji, Naraka, Marvel Rivals (licensed). Top-tier R&D team. Brand cachet among Chinese gamers as "quality-first" publisher.

2. Switching Costs / 转换成本 — 3/3

MMORPGs have among the highest switching costs in gaming: years of character progression, social bonds, guild membership, sunk-cost investment. NetEase's legacy titles retain players for 10-20+ years.

3. Network Effects / 网络效应 — 2/3

Strong within individual game ecosystems (guilds, social features, UGC). Cloud Music has 200M+ MAU with community features. Weaker cross-product network effects vs Tencent's WeChat ecosystem.

4. Cost Advantages / 成本优势 — 2/3

One of the largest in-house game R&D teams globally. Proprietary NeoX and Messiah engines reduce reliance on 3rd-party tools. AI integration (CodeMaker, DreamMaker) accelerating production. Scale advantage in China's massive market.

5. Efficient Scale / 规模效益 — 1/3

Gaming industry is large and competitive; no monopoly-like scale benefit. However, duopoly with Tencent in China's regulated market provides some protection. License requirements create barriers to entry.
