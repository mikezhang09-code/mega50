§ 02 · Business Overview

What the Company Does 业务概览

Designs and manufactures high-precision CNC machine tools — primarily small-diameter automatic lathes — at a facility in Pinghu, Zhejiang. Sells capital equipment to automotive, electronics, medical, industrial automation, and newly emerging AI/humanoid-robot customers across China and internationally.

Why Tsugami (China)? 为什么选择津上

The company's strategic position is unusual: a Japanese precision-engineering brand (Tsugami Japan, founded 1937) operating a fully Chinese manufacturing base. Customers get the quality and reliability of Japanese-engineered machines at a 30–50% cost discount to importing from Japan. This has allowed PTC to rank #1 among foreign-owned CNC machine-tool makers in China by revenue — a position that has compounded advantages in distribution, service network, and reference installations.
