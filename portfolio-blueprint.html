China Risk Factors
中国投资风险

⚠ VIE Structure Risk

US investors own Cayman shells bound by contracts, not actual Chinese equity. Government could theoretically invalidate. Mitigated by buying HK-listed shares.

⚠ Delisting Risk

~280 Chinese companies ($880B market cap) trade in the US. US officials have said delisting is "on the table." 75% by cap also trade in HK as a fallback.

⚠ Geopolitical Tail Risk

Taiwan tensions, trade wars, and regulatory unpredictability are permanent features. The valuation discount is the compensation for bearing these risks.

Capital Deployment Strategy
资金部署策略

Month 1 — Deploy 60% ($60,000)

Lump-sum invest the majority immediately. Vanguard's research shows lump-sum beats DCA in 67% of historical periods, delivering an average 2.4% higher return. Markets rise ~70-75% of all 12-month periods.

Months 2–6 — DCA Remaining 25% ($25,000)

Dollar-cost average into India and HK/China positions during volatility. Target dips in FLIN, INDA, MCHI, and individual HK stocks. Given elevated US CAPE (~36.65), spreading international buys smooths entry risk.

Months 6–12 — Deploy Final 15% ($15,000)

Complete bond, REIT, and gold allocations. Keep $4,000 in high-yield savings (4.0–4.2% APY) as permanent dry-powder reserve for opportunistic rebalancing.

Ongoing — Annual Rebalance + 5% Drift Trigger

Conduct scheduled annual review. Also rebalance if any asset drifts more than 5 percentage points from target. This hybrid approach captured 38bps of additional annual return in Morgan Stanley's research.

Tax Optimization Placement
税务优化建议

✓ Taxable Account

International ETFs (MCHI, FLIN, INDA) to capture Foreign Tax Credit. HK charges 0% withholding — most tax-friendly market for US investors. US equities (VOO, VTI, individual stocks) for long-term capital gains rates.

✓ Tax-Advantaged (IRA/401k)

Bonds (BND, TIP) — interest taxed as ordinary income. REITs (VNQ) — distributions taxed as ordinary income. Gold (IAU) — taxed at 28% collectibles rate. India stocks (IBN, HDB) — 25% withholding not recoverable.
