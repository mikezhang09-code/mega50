⚠ The Panama Ports Situation 巴拿馬港口危機

Critical issue for valuation. On January 29, 2026, Panama's Supreme Court annulled CK Hutchison's Balboa and Cristóbal port concessions as unconstitutional. Panama formally seized the ports on February 23, 2026, with Maersk (Balboa) and MSC/TiL (Cristóbal) taking interim operating control. CK Hutchison has initiated international arbitration; legal experts expect a multi-year battle with uncertain compensation. 巴拿馬最高法院已於2026年1月29日裁定港口特許違憲，兩港口於2月23日被政府接管。
