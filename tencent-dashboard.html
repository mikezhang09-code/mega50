Reverse DCF — What's Priced In? 反向DCF分析

At HKD 499, backing out the investment portfolio (~RMB 1,036B), the core business is valued at ~RMB 3,122B against RMB 260B owner earnings = 12× earnings.

This implies the market is pricing ~4-5% perpetual growth — far below Tencent's demonstrated 14-17% trajectory. The market is essentially valuing a world-class tech platform as a no-growth utility.
