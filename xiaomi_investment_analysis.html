Section 03

Moat Assessment 护城河分析

Xiaomi has successfully built a Narrow to Wide Moat based on network effects, ecosystem lock-in, and scale advantages.

  • Network Effects & Switching Costs (Strong): Users buying into Xiaomi's HyperOS ecosystem face increasing switching costs. Moving from a Xiaomi phone to a Xiaomi EV integrated with home IoT devices creates immense lifestyle stickiness.
  • Cost Advantage (Moderate): Xiaomi’s legendary supply chain efficiency, initially built around low-margin hardware, is paying compounding dividends in EV manufacturing scale.
  • Brand Intangibles (Strong): Despite early impressions as a "budget" choice, Xiaomi effectively commands premium status in China alongside Apple and Huawei, further validated by its rapid EV execution.
Section 04

Intrinsic Valuation 估值分析

At a market cap of HK$800 billion (~RMB 740B), Xiaomi is trading at roughly 18.8x its FY25 adjusted net profit of RMB 39.2 billion. Given its EV trajectory and historical >15% bottom-line CAGR, this is a highly attractive multiple for a global tech compounding machine.

Section 05

Investment Decision 投资结论

ACCUMULATE

A Tech Powerhouse at a Fair Price

Xiaomi merits an ACCUMULATE rating. The company has executed almost flawlessly on the hardest pivot in modern hardware: seamlessly entering the EV space. With its "Human x Car x Home" strategy generating real operating profits, a 24% EV margin, and Internet Services providing a high-margin bedrock, Xiaomi is competitively entrenched. The current price (around HK$31) provides an adequate margin of safety for long-term investors looking for secular Chinese tech growth combined with automotive manufacturing leverage.
